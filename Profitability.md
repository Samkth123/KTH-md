
Profitability is falling IF:
- Revenue is declining?
	- Quantity of goods/services sold
	- Average selling price
- Costs are increasing?
	- Fixed costs
	- Variable costs

OEM profitability falling due to:
- Revenue is declining?
	- Quantity and product mix of assemblies sold (e.g., more sales of less profitable item)
	- Average price (e.g., discounts to new customer?)
- Costs are increasing?
	- Fixed costs (e.g., manufacturing plants, equipment, new investment needed for new customer - equipment, R&D, etc.?)
	- Variable costs (e.g., raw goods, labor, supplier changes?)
